Corporates

Membership to Rununu sacco is open to all Kenyans of all walks of life salaried or self employed, resident in Kenya or out of the country. The following table enumerates joining fees for each category.


Category Joining One off Initial Shares Total to pay Minimum monthly contribution
Corporate Sh. 10,000 Sh. 5,000 Sh. 15,000 Sh. 3,000

PROCEDURE

Be introduced by an existing member or register your self on your phone by writing an sms “rununu sr#group name# reg no#” send to 22384 and then follow the prompts. You can also go here fill in the form and follow the prompts. After you have paid the initial to pay you may continue buying shares as well as recruiting new members at a commission. Remember to upload your company details and download your member’s card at a later date. Please note that you shall not qualify for any product if you have not uploaded your details.

What will you upload? 1) pictures of 3-4 officials, 2)both sides of your IDs 3) you will fill in your names, tel no, box no, email address, county, constituency/sub county,ward, 4) upload your certificate of registration 4) signed resolution to join rununu 5) your constitution NB. All these must be notarized.
